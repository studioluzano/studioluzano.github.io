ABOUT THE CLASS

Observation Drawing Basics is a beginner-friendly observation drawing class held in my studio in Kruununhaka, Helsinki. The focus is on drawing from real objects, using your own eyes and hands, and learning to slow down, observe, and make intentional marks. These sessions support the local art community and are designed to keep learning accessible.

You’ll learn how to:

  • Simplify objects into basic shapes and proportions
  • Understand how light creates form
  • Draw with clarity instead of guesswork
  • Make calm, confident, and intentional marks

PRICE & BOOKING

Whether you’re starting from scratch or rediscovering drawing, these sessions are designed to be relaxed, personal, and beginner-friendly.
