About the Class

Studio Luzano Koulu – Observation Drawing Basics is designed for anyone who wants to learn how to truly see what’s in front of them and translate it onto paper. No previous experience is required; curiosity and patience are enough.

Classes are calm and structured, with a focus on light, form, shape clarity and building confidence in your mark-making.
