About the Class

Observation Drawing Basics is a gentle introduction to learning to see like an artist. We take things slowly: noticing simple shapes, understanding light, and building confidence through relaxed drawing exercises. No experience needed, just curiosity! Each session is beginner-friendly, with personal guidance.

You'll learn: How to simplify objects into basic shapes and proportions How light creates form How to draw with clarity instead of guesswork How to make confident, intentional marks.
