RUN

Selected spreads and panels from RUN, a graphic novel written by Andrew Katz and created with Bradley Heaven & Daniel O’Connor. RUN is an intimate portrait of platonic soulmates Bradley Heaven—who lives with Dyskinetic Cerebral Palsy and is non-verbal—and Daniel O’Connor, his aide, business partner and best friend, as they seek to fulfill a long-time dream: run a marathon together. Coming 2027.

A key emotional moment: the race, the crowd, and a difficult decision.
