About

I’m a Montréal-born illustrator and concept artist now based in Helsinki. I bring over ten years of experience in animation and publishing, with a focus on expressive storytelling and atmospheric worldbuilding.

My work spans children’s books, graphic novels and animated TV series. I enjoy adapting to different styles and shaping ideas into clear, emotional images.

Outside of client work, I teach observation-based drawing at my studio in Kruununhaka, Helsinki.
