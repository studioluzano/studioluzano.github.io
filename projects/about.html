About

I’m a Montréal-born illustrator and concept artist now based in Helsinki. I bring over nine years of experience in animation and publishing, with a focus on lighting, colour, and atmosphere.”.

My work spans children’s books and animated TV and commercial. I enjoy finding the visual language that best carries the story at hand.

Outside of client work, I teach drawing classes at my studio in Kruununhaka, Helsinki.
