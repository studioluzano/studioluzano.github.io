About

I’m a Montréal-born illustrator based in Helsinki. My work is defined by expressive mark-making, rich texture, and a quiet emotional tone.

I collaborate with publishers, authors and creative teams on literary works. With over ten years of experience in illustration, and work spanning both animation and literary contexts, I approach each piece with a strong sense of narrative, composition and world beyond the frame.

Outside of client work, I teach observational drawing at my studio.

I’m always open to illustration projects for literary works, books, editorial pieces, and print, feel free to get in touch.
