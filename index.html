Koulu · Observation drawing classes

I teach beginner-friendly drawing classes in my studio located in Kruununhaka, Helsinki. We focus on developing the skills to draw from observation, and being able to see like an artist, in a relaxed environment.

About

I’m a Montréal-born concept artist and illustrator now working in Helsinki, with a decade of experience in animation, publishing, and visual storytelling. I’m known for adapting to many styles and bringing ideas to life across TV productions, children’s books, and graphic novels.
